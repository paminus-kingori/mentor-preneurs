Introduction to Mentor-preneurs Organization

Mentor-Preneurs Organization (M-PO) is an organization with the sole objective of providing avenue to the mentorship of Youth in entrepreneurial skills.

Mentor-Preneurship is a process of mentoring the young entrepreneurs by building their skills, network and creating an enabling environment for growth. It is a process that brings a mentor and a mentee to interact and facilitate the skill build up in entrepreneurship.

The organization creates an avenue for the established entrepreneurs to not only give back to the community but also to mentor a youth in the community into entrepreneurship. The engagement process is structured and defined with specific measurable targets by both parties.

Vision:

Transformed Generation of Youth Entrepreneurs

Mission:

To create a transformed community of young entrepreneurs through mentorship, skills and experiences sharing upholding Christ principles.
